Cough Information

Types of Cough:
  • 1. Dry Cough
  • 2. Wet or Productive Cough
  • 3. Chronic Cough

Common Medicines:

Depending on the type of cough, different medicines may be recommended:

  • For Dry Cough: You can consider using cough suppressants like Dextromethorphan.
  • For Wet or Productive Cough: Expectorants like Guaifenesin can help loosen mucus.
  • For Chronic Cough: Consult a doctor for proper diagnosis and treatment options.

Suggestions for Managing Cough:

  • 1. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • 2. Use a humidifier to add moisture to the air.
  • 3. Avoid irritants like smoke and allergens.
  • 4. Get plenty of rest to help your body heal.
  • 5. If cough persists or worsens, consult a healthcare professional.

Please note that this information is for general guidance. Always consult a healthcare provider for personalized advice.
