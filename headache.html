Headache

Overview

A common symptom that causes pain and discomfort in the head or neck region.

Medication

  • Acetaminophen (Tylenol)
  • Ibuprofen (Advil,mortin)
  • Aspirin

Home Remedies

In addition to medication, you can try yhe following home remedies to relieve headache:

  • Rest in a dark and quiet room
  • Apply a cold or warm compress to your forehead
  • stay hydrated by drinkimg plenty of water
  • practice relaxation techniques,such as deep breathing

Prevention

  • Avoid triggers that may cause your headaches
  • maintain a regular sleep schedule
  • Stay physically active
  • manage a stress through meditation or yoga

    • Remember, it's crucial to follow your healthcare provider's recommendations for the most effective treatment.
