Fever Information

Overview

Common Cause Of Fever

  • Viruses (e.g., flu or cold)

  • Bacterial Infection

  • Inflammatory Condition

  • Heat Exhaustion

  • Skin Information

  • Gastrointestinal (GI) infections

Suggestions for Managing Fever

  • Get plenty of rest.

  • Stay hydrated by drinking water,clear broths,or electrolyte drinks.

  • Use a damp cloth to cool your forehead and body.

  • Avoid alcohol and caffeine.

  • If the Fevre persists or worsens , seek medical attention

Remember, it's crucial to follow your healthcare provider's recommendations for the most effective treatment.
